White chooses Zakharov's plan, just like after 11...Bd7 . 12.h4!? , 12.Kb1 , 12.Rhe1 and 12.g2-g4 have also been played - see the next game.

Summary of 11...a6 12.Ne5 : A flank attack should be met by an attack in the centre! Following this classical concept, Black played ...a6/...Bd6/...Qc7, and defended his position against Zakharov's dangerous plan. After this White did not have enough time or resources to attack the opponent's king.

1/2-1/2

Summary of the 'Salto Mortale' 9.Nf4 : It is obvious that White does not have enough resources to unleash a serious attack. After a forced sequence of moves, the resulting positions are balanced, but the material is not. The advantage will be on the side of the player who has analysed these positions better and understands them more deeply Key moments: Black can choose from three defences: 9...Qe7 . In the main line, after 9...Nxd4! 10.Qh5+ Ke7 , the move 11.Ng6+?! seems to be a mistake ( 11.exf6+! is better), and 15...e4!? is useful for its surprise value, but it probably leads to a forced draw, while 15...Nxf3 is more flexible and offers many possibilities to both sides.

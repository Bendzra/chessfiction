The French Defense is one of the most resilient and solid defenses Black can play and it is effective at all levels of chess skill. So White is constantly looking for ways to meet it. This book will fulfill that search. Covered are: 1) The Devious Alapin-Diemer Gambit 1.e4 e6 2.d4 d5 3.Be3 then on 3..fxe4 4.f3; 2) The Tricky French Wing Gambit; 3) The Murderous Milner-Barry in the Advance Variation(6.Bd3); 4) Winckelmann-Reimer, The IM-Killer(in the Winawer Variation) and an extra chapter on extra gambits.
